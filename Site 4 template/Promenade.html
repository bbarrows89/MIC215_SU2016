"Promenade" is a vintage-inspired wedding dress made from 100% silk shantung. The bodice has waist and bust darts, and has a wide Sabrina neckline. The back is open but is still bra-friendly, the neckline is trimmed with beautiful rhinestone chain, and the back bow is removable.

This dress has a gathered skirt and a zipper back, and is fully lined. Rhinestones are glass. The skirt is underlined in silk and has a traditional couture hand-finished horsehair braid hem rather than a machine hem.​

PETTICOAT: This dress can be worn with or without petticoat. I suggest wearing a petticoat that is 1-2" shorter than the skirt. I have listed several petticoat suppliers on my "links" page. The dress is pictured here with the MM-582 petticoat in white.
