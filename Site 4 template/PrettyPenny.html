​"Pretty Penny" is a strapless wedding dress made from white cotton eyelet inspired by classic vintage bridal styles.

With a crumbcatcher neckline and gathered skirt, this dress is held up by an inner corselette fully boned with spiral steel boning. I finished the back closure with corset-style grommets with satin ribbon lacing for an adjustable fit. There is a short zipper in the skirt below the grommets. The skirt of this dress has a double lining.  The skirt is 29" from waist to hem.

This dress may be worn with or without a petticoat.  I suggest wearing a petticoat that is 1-2" shorter than the skirt.  I have listed several petticoat suppliers on my "links" page.
