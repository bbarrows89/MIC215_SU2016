​"Chandelier" is a 1930's inspired long bias-cut wedding gown. The graceful, open back is weighted with a long clear crystal on a chain.  This dress is available in either polyester crepe or silk crepe.

The bodice is cut on the bias and lined with oyster colored charmeuse. The back panels are also bias-cut and drape gracefully at the waistline. This gown is fully lined in oyster charmeuse.  Available with roses at shoulders or loops.
