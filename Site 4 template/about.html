I am Colleen Thorsen, and I was born and raised in the Seattle area.  The youngest of 8 children, I learned most of my sewing skills from my mother, Ida, and my five older sisters.

In 2005 after taking on the project of making all of the bridesmaid dresses for a wedding, I became interested in the art of couture, and in 2006 received my Certificate of Couture Sewing Techniques.

Since then I have been sewing my unique styles, inspired mostly by vintage silhouettes.  My demi-couture dresses are available online at Etsy.com.

Welcome!  I am Colleen Thorsen, owner of French Knot Couture.  Together with my sister, Dorothy, we specialize in wedding dresses in eyelets and floral prints.  We have a collection of tea length dresses as well as a collection of long gowns, and each dress is handmade in our studio.

Our dresses are available for purchase on ETSY.com.

We will be adding a selection of handmade couture tweed and boucle French jackets very soon. We look forward to making them available!
