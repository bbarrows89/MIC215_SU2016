How to Order a Dress

French Knot Couture offers dresses in standard sizes for purchase online at Etsy.com.  These dresses are made when ordered and shipped out as soon as completed.  Worldwide shipping is available.  

Lead times vary during the year, so you will want to check my Etsy shop for current time frames. 

​For standard sized dresses alterations are the responsibility of the buyer. For more details on payment/shipping/returns, please see the "Policies" page of my Etsy shop.

Muslin Service for Local Brides

If you are a Seattle area bride interested in one of my dresses, and you want to ensure a personalized fit ... this is for you.

A "muslin" is a cotton fabric mock-up of your dress. The muslin is used for fitting adjustments, and ultimately becomes the pattern for your dress.

French Knot Muslin Service includes consultation, measurements by Colleen, muslin construction and alterations. Please expect 3 - 5 fittings in my workroom before delivery of your dress.
