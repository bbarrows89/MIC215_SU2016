Sweet Summertime is a tea-length wedding dress in white eyelet.  It has a Sabrina neckline with a very low back that ties.

The bodice is darted, and the neckline is high.  It has a full skirt and zipper in the center back.  Fully lined.  The skirt length shown in the last 2 pictures is 28" from waist to hem.  Standard length is 25", but you may specify length when ordering.

​Sweet Summertime is available in several different eyelet patterns.  White satin belt with rhinestone buckle is included.

This dress may be worn with or without a petticoat.  I suggest wearing a petticoat that is 1-2" shorter than the skirt.  I have listed several petticoat suppliers on my "links" page.
