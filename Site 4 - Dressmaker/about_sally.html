About Sally Dressmaker

Behind the best wedding dresses of Seattle, and even the world, Sally Dressmaker is a legend in the bridal community. She is proud to offer her services to Seattle and the Pacific Northwest.

Sally got her start in New York City, where she had the pleasure of studying under Christian Dior at the age of five. She was surrounded by fashion from birth, her mother being a famous designer in her own right.

When she was 18, she was accepted to the Rhode Island School of Design, where she majored in Wedding Dress Creation. In her time at RISD, she refined her techniques and by the age of 21, was featured in a major New York Times piece, highlighting her work as the "future of the industry".

Since then, she has revolutionized the world of wedding dresses and her inspiration can be seen everywhere.
